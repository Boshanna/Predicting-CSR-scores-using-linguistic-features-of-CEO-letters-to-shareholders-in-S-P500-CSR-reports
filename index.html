1. A short description of CSR data that I will be using in my project, and the objective of the data analysis.

The paper compares the performance of eleven statistical and mahcine learning models in predicting CSR performance score that is provided by Bloomberg based on various linguistic features in CEO letter to shareholders in CSR reporting. I conduct my investigating utilizing a sample from the large US companies that are listed in the S&P 500 index. The sample includes 176 companies in 2015 (cross sectional data). Restricting the sample to companies in the S&P 500 is because S&P 500 is widely used in CSR research (Lougee and Wallace, 2008; Liston-Heyes & Ceton, 2009; Tayler et al., 2018). Also, S&P 500 index contains the majority of large companies that are operating in the United States. I subsequently hand collected the companies’ CEO letter to shareholders in 2015 via several steps. First, I downloaded S&P 500 companies’ CSR reports that were available on Bloomberg during my sample period. I used additional sources when data were not available on Bloomberg and these sources included company websites, the, and Edgar. Second, I manually had exported the CEO letter from the CSR report and posted on a word file. Afterword, For CEO letter, the construction of readability indices requires sample texts. I omit tables, bulleted lists, and headings because readability indices are designed for sentence-format text. I run CEOs letters into Coh- Metrix that is available at http://cohmetrix.com/ I obtained the following linguistic features from Coh-metrix tool:

  1. Number of paragraphs: DESPC

  2. Number of sentences: DESSC

  3. Number of words: DESWC

  4. Paragraph length, number of sentences, mean: DESPL

  5. Paragraph length, number of sentences, standard deviation: DESPLd

  6. Sentence length, number of words, mean: DESSL

  7. Sentence length, number of words, standard deviation: DESSLd

  8. Word length, number of syllables, mean: DESWLsy

  9. Word length, number of syllables, standard deviation: DESWLsyd

  10. Word length, number of letters, mean: DESWLlt

  11. Word length, number of letters, standard deviation: DESWLltd

  12. Narrativity: PCNARp

  13. Syntactic Simplicity: PCSYNp,

  14. Word Concreteness: PCCNCp.

  15. Referential Cohesion: PCREFp.

  16. Deep Cohesion: PCDCp.

  17. Verb Cohesion: PCVERBp.

  18. Connectivity: PCCONNp.

  19. Temporality: PCTEMPp.

  20. Flesch Reading Ease: RDFRE.

  21. Flesch_Kincaid Grade Level: RDFKGL

  22. Posemo: postiive tone, I use LIWC tool

  23. Negemo: negative tone, I use LIWC tool

The a table of definition of each variable/feature can be found on the link below: http://cohmetrix.com/
